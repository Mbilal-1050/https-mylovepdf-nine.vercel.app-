How to Merge PDF Files in 3 Easy Steps

📅 January 15, 2025 ⏱ 3 min read ✍️ PDFMaster Team
Advertisement

Need to combine multiple PDF files into one? Whether you're merging reports, contracts, or presentations, PDFMaster makes it incredibly simple — and completely free.

In this guide, we'll show you exactly how to merge PDF files in just 3 steps using our free online tool. No software download needed.

Step 1: Upload Your PDF Files

Go to the Merge PDF tool on PDFMaster. Click the "Choose PDF Files" button or drag and drop your files directly onto the upload area. You can upload multiple PDFs at once.

💡 Pro Tip: Hold Ctrl (Windows) or Cmd (Mac) to select multiple files at once in the file browser.

Step 2: Arrange the Order

After uploading, you can drag and drop the files to arrange them in the exact order you want in the merged PDF. The order shown on screen is the order they'll appear in the final document.

Step 3: Click Merge & Download

Click the "Merge PDF Files" button. Our servers will combine your files in seconds. Once done, click Download to save your merged PDF. That's it!
